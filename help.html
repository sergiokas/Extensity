Now, the long version for those interested:

I've started Extensity as a hobby project a few years ago, and it's now being used by hundreds of thousands of people around the world. That was a humbling experience, but it also comes with the responsibility of keeping it up-to-date and listening to users when they have problems or request new features.

However, I want to keep Extensity, with all its features, free of charge for everyone.

For that reason, Extensity will never include ads, will not release a paid PRO version, won't include in-app purchases, and won't track its users behavior for profit.

One of the most requested features was the ability to turn several extensions on and off with a single click. It's called "Profiles", and it's now released. Automatic synchronization of your settings between computers is supported out of the box.

Please consider making a donation to fund the time invested in its development.
